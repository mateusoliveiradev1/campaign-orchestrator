> **Turn one strategy document into a fully mapped, budget-allocated campaign.**

Marketing managers spend days translating a brief into channel-specific assets, spreadsheet budget splits, and UTM structures. Something always gets missed. This agent extracts your core message, **calculates budget splits deterministically via Python**, and outputs a MECE asset pipeline across every channel simultaneously.

### ✨ Enterprise Features
* **Deterministic Budget Allocation:** `budget_allocator.py` splits your total spend across Meta/IG (50%), Search (30%), and LinkedIn (20%) with exact dollar amounts — no back-of-napkin math.
* **MECE Asset Pipeline:** A complete, non-overlapping list of every copy asset needed for Email, Social, and Paid Search.
* **UTM Taxonomy:** A structured table of tracking links for clean attribution from day one.
* **Single Source of Truth:** One brief in, one complete campaign blueprint out.
